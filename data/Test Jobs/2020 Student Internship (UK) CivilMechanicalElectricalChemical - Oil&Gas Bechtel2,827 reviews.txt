Bechtel is one of the most respected global engineering, construction and project management companies, and a cornerstone of innovation in the industry. Together with our customers, we deliver landmark projects that foster sustainable progress and grow economies.

Our 10-week Infrastructure Internship Scheme is open to all undergraduates in the recruiting disciplines we have openings for. In most cases, you’ll be assigned to a project or placed in an office where you can support our day-to-day operations. During this time you’ll see how our business works and take on tasks that will give you valuable experience – all while having a real impact on our organisation.

You will have a dedicated supervisor who will be responsible for providing you with a full and busy workload and ensuring we provide you with the best experience possible during your time with us.

Setting personal goals to help you get the most out of your internshipWorking with a dedicated supervisor to ensure your workload challenges youAccess to a variety of social events, such as Royal Ascot, Sports Day and much moreExposure to senior management through lunch and learn sessions and Q&As
We accept applications from students who are in their penultimate year of study (2nd year of a 3 year degree or 3rd year of a 4 year degree). All our applicants must be on course for achieving a 2:1 degree or equivalent. We will be recruiting interns for the below areas:
