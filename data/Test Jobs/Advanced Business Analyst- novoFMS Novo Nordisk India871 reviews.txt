About the department
Global IT India was established in February 2010 as an integral part of Global IT in HQ. From the beginning in 2010, the unit has grown to 300+ employees till date. The main role of Global IT India is to 'manage' IT, which includes System Management, Project Management, Infrastructure Management, Compliance Management, Security Management, Process Management and Vendor Management.

The position
The primary responsibility of the position is to manage Novo Facility Monitoring System (FMS) and Novo Building Monitoring System (BMS) systems by performing the roles such as Incident Manager, Problem Manager, Change Manager and Validation Responsible.
The crucial responsibilities related to this role are yearly system management activities, excellence in business and system understanding. You will demonstrate pro-activeness/ownership with high level of engagement and management of stakeholder/vendor. We expect you to actively participate in the system management forums and project management related activities. You will also support implementation activities, remote support to end users globally, potential onsite support for deployments as required and follow agile principles and methodologies.
