Analyst - Analytics
Relocation Assistance Offered Within Country
# 81680 - Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
The incumbent will be responsible for supporting Colgate’s data management processes and standard reporting. In addition, conduct first level analysis of reports for Colgate business functions at global, division and hub (country clusters) levels. The incumbent must be an analytic thinker with the ability to work with large data sets, detail-oriented, problem solver, collaborative and customer-oriented (proactive and responsive to stakeholder needs).

Primary Responsibility
Create standard reports per agreed to SLAs and respond to ad hoc requests for reporting and first level analysis.
Supports market data harmonization process set up and business as usual across all CP/Hill’s countries and categories working with external strategic partners as appropriate.
Manage market data validation processes and tools and investigate significant differences from what was previously reported.
Communicate and coordinate with Divisions and subsidiaries as part of investigation and resolution of discrepancies.
Update and manage master data sets.
Assist with change management needs (e.g., training, communications, etc.) as part of roll-out and ongoing support of market data reporting tools and processes.
Ready to work closely with business partners across geographies

Performance / Skill Criteria
Mandatory
Intermediate knowledge of Excel (i.e., standard functions – aggregation, pivots, look ups, etc.)
Intermediate Knowledge of data processing and visualization tools (R, Python & Tableau )
Good understanding of Trade – Direct, Indirect & Retail, Distribution Models
High level of comfort with large sets of data and attend to detail s and passion for accuracy
Problem-solver, with analytic thinking and ability to apply root cause analysis
Ability to prioritize multiple work assignments and meet deadlines

Good to Have
Experience with third-party data i.e., syndicated market data, Point of Sales, etc.
Working knowledge of consumer packaged goods industry
Understanding of Colgate’s/Hill’s processes, and tools supporting analytics (for internal candidates)
Willingness and ability to experiment with new tools and techniques
Good facilitation and project management skills
Ability to maintain personal composure and tactfully handle difficult situations.

Required Experience / Education
Bachelor of Engineering / Sciences
4+ years of experience
Proficient in Reporting and/or Budgeting
