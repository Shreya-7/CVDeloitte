Business Analyst (OIPA)

Equisoft is searching for a hands-on business analyst who will be responsible for requirements gathering, functional analysis, as well as support in planning and coordination of project activities.

Working as part of a project team, the business analyst will work closely with clients and act as liaison between the project team and the client. The analyst will be required to gather formal requirements from the clients and translate those requirements into clearly defined deliverables for the implementation team. The business analyst is expected to become a subject matter expert in the area of insurance as well as in the Equisoft platform being implemented.
If you have experience in the insurance industry, enjoy new technologies, are eager to optimize and simplify business processes, and can explain complex notions to non-technical people and key stakeholders, send us your resume!

A DAY IN YOUR LIFE
Drive requirement sessions with the client around policy administration business processes, technical processes, and non-functional requirements

Translate insurance product requirements into business and functional requirements, and translate insurance business processes, market practices, and other client business needs into specific software requirements

Work with the team leads and client Subject Matter Experts to identify and document solutions, define the project scope, project estimates and timelines

Present and recommend solutions to clients that meet their business needs

Support the project manager and development team by owning the documentation of the detailed business requirements, sprint planning, backlog grooming, and identifying out of scope requirements and change requests

MUST-HAVES

University Degree or commensurate experience, and the ability to communicate, write and synthesize information. Formal business analysis experience is a definite asset

Comprehensive understanding of Life and Health insurance products and processes. Understanding of group insurance is an asset

Experience working with insurance policy administration systems, e-applications, or illustrations, and the ability to learn how insurance products and processes are implemented on one or several of Equisoft’s platforms

Experience with OIPA (Oracle Insurance Policy Administration) or Equisoft products is an asset

Strong organizational skills with a proven ability to prioritize activities effectively

Excellent knowledge of English (spoken and written). Knowledge of Spanish or French is an asset.

Understanding of the software development cycle. Some technical knowledge is an asset

Ability to travel domestically and/or internationally

YOUR BENEFITS

Competitive compensation

Comprehensive benefits packages

International assignments


ABOUT EQUISOFT

Founded in 1994, Equisoft is a global provider of advanced digital solutions in life insurance and wealth management. Recognized as a valued partner by over 50 of the world’s leading financial institutions in 15 countries, Equisoft offers innovative front-end applications, extensive back-office services and a unique data migration expertise. The firm’s industry-leading products include a comprehensive SaaS policy administration solution, CRM, financial needs analysis, asset allocation, quotes and illustrations, electronic application, agency management systems, as well as customer, agent and broker portals. Equisoft is also Oracle’s main global partner for the Oracle Insurance Policy Administration platform. With its business-driven approach, deep industry knowledge, state-of-the-art technology, and over 450 specialized resources based in the USA, Canada, Latin America, South Africa, India and Australia, Equisoft helps its clients tackle any challenge in this era of digital disruption.
