High Level Function and Role Overview
The purpose of Operations IT is to partner with the global AstraZeneca Operations organization to deliver excellent Information Technology to support and accelerate business objectives.
The team in which this role is based is customer-facing and strong partnering and influencing skills are essential. As a Business Analyst, you will work closely with your customer groups to understand their key challenges and use your knowledge and experience to determine where IM and/or IT would provide optimal value including:
Ensuring proposed and delivered solutions meet business needs Proactively seeking opportunities in IT that might benefit your business customers Providing support & consultancy to users on system exploitation and best practice to maximise value from existing IT solutions
Whilst this role is not a hands-on technical IT role, to be successful as a Business Analyst in AstraZeneca, you will have a demonstrable appreciation of current technology trends and a strong desire to improve the IT user experience in the business. SAP and Ariba suites form the underlying technology driving the core business processes and these are supplemented by a myriad of other solutions providing differentiated, supporting solutions for more bespoke business processes.
