Business Analyst
We are looking for a Business Analysts who will be responsible for documentation and assume the overall responsibility for the collection and consolidation of required information and data. He should be able to write business process documents using software tools. The Business Analyst will also be responsible for managing client relationships while coordinating project activities. The successful candidate will have a meticulous attention to detail, exceptional written and verbal communication skills, and be able to provide technical documentation.
Job Description
At least 3 years of experience in business process engineering, business system analysis and working through the SDLC,
Experience writing functional requirements specification (FRS).
Experience writing business process documents using BPM tools
Excellent communication, both verbal and written.
Strong requirements gathering skills (interviewing business and operational users).
Good at writing Functional Specifications , Business Flow and Requirements.
Basic understanding of the company’s business practices and familiarity with the company’s products and services.
Good interpersonal skills and excellent organizational skills.
Candidate Profile
Any graduate with degree in Business Management
2 to 3 years of experience as a Business Analyst - Software
Should be well aware of SDLC, Agile development Technology
Good at Business Systems analysis, FRS, FRD’s, requirement gathering
Usage BPM tools – Must
Excellent written and verbal communication skills, able to facilitate communication upwards and downwards
Compensation
The position will pay a competitive salary (industry standards) plus opportunity for bonus compensation and travelling allowance.
The initial CTC will be based on the previous CTC of the candidate and later it will be revised based on the performance
