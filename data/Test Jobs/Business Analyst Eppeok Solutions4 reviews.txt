Business Analyst
key responsibilities
Must be responsible for Client Acquisition, Client Reference, Marketing, Generate leads.
Manage to get projects (International & Domestic), from different platform and other ways.
Determine operational objectives by studying business functions; gathering information; evaluating output requirements and formats.
Improve the system by studying current practices; designing modifications.
Recommend controls by identifying problems; writing improved procedures.
Monitor project progress by tracking activity; resolving problems; publishing progress reports; recommending actions.
Maintain user confidence and protect operations by keeping information confidential.
Prepare technical reports by collecting, analysing and summarizing information and trends.
Review a variety of areas including operations, distribution etc.
Understand and communicate the financial and operational impact of any changes.
Actively participate in the implementation of approved changes.
Create informative, actionable and repeatable reporting that highlights relevant business trends and opportunities for improvement.
Essential Skill set/s:
Business Development Management, Marketing, Lead Generation, Client Acquisition, Bidding, IT Business Analyst, Senior Business Analyst, Documentation, Strategy, Senior Management, Budgeting, Operations, Market Research.
Desirable Skill set/s:
Must have good knowledge in Business Development, Bidding.
Excellent communication skill.

UG: B.Tech/B.E. in Any Specialization, Computers, Graduation Not Required, Any Graduate in Any Specialization, B.Sc in Any Specialization, Computers, B.Com in Commerce.

PG: Post Graduation Not Required, Any Postgraduate in Any Specialization

Doctorate: Doctorate Not Required, Any Doctorate in Any Specialization
Work Experience:
3-5 years of relevant experience required.

At least 3 Years of experience in the field of Business Analysis in any IT service-based organisation.
