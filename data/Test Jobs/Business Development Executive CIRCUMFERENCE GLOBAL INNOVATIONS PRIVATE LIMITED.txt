Business Development Executive
Summary: The Business Development Executive is responsible for generating leads, advancing the sales process, closing new business and achieving/assisting in the achievement of opportunity-based sales quotas. Listening to customer requirements and presenting appropriately to make a sale along with the influencers (Architects/Interior Designers).Job Duties: Develop sales opportunities by researching and identifying potential accounts.Penetrate all targeted accounts and radiate sales from within client base.Collaborate with appropriate team members to determine necessary strategic sales approaches.Cold call prospects that are generated by external sources of lead.Create and deliver qualified opportunities to other team members.Maintain and expand the company’s database of prospects.Set up and deliver sales presentations, product/service demonstrations, and other sales actions.Where necessary, support marketing efforts such as trade shows, exhibits, and other events. Whenever necessary, the executive may need to travel to client locations across India.Make outbound follow-up calls to existing clients via telephone and email cross-sell and up­sell.Handle inbound, unsolicited prospect calls and convert into sales. Negotiating the terms of the agreement and closing sales.Emphasize product/service features and benefits, quote prices, discuss credit terms and prepare sales order forms and/or reports.Enter new customer data and update changes to existing accounts in the corporate database.Appropriately communicate a brand identity and corporate position.Job Types: Full-time, FresherSalary: ₹15,000.00 to ₹30,000.00 /monthEducation:Bachelor's (Preferred)Travel requirements:Yes - ~25%Industry:Architecture
