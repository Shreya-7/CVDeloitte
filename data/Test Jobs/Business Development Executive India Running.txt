Business Development Executive
Job Type: Full Time

Location: Remote/ Work from Home

Remuneration: Base Rs.21,000 - 30,000 per month + Commission

Your job: The Business Development Executive must have a proven ability to multitask, collaborate, and communicate with clients effectively. A positive, flexible, can-do attitude is a must, as is the ability to turn around quickly. The ideal candidate for this role is a self-starter who can balance competing priorities. If you have a passion for delivering measurable results, we want to hear from you.


Responsibilities:
Responsible for coordinating with the existing as well as new clients of the company.
Identifying and establishing contact with potential customers proactively.
Achieving sales and revenue targets in assigned territory.
Solving clients problems, queries to retain the assigned accounts.
Responding to inquiries from end customers.
Thorough business planning and knowledge on competitor analysis.
Provide required reports and information to clients.
Following up new business opportunities and setting up meetings.
Planning and preparing presentations.
Communicating new product developments to prospective clients.

Who can apply:
Must have excellent communication skills in English
Must have experience with MS Office Presentation, Word and Excel
Can-do attitude with a service-oriented approach.
Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment.

Preferred:
Running/ cycling event organizer.
People with good relationship with local organizers.
People with experience with advertising industry.
