Department Overview
Capital Markets Technology is within the Enterprise Information Technology business and the portfolio consists of Technology solutions for Primary Markets /Investment Banking/GIB Municipal Products/Asset Backed Finance, various Secondary Markets Trading verticals (like Equities, Fixed Income, and Commodities etc.) and Institutional Investor Services (like Prime Brokerage, Futures Commission Merchant Services).

About the Role
Capital Market Business Analyst who can interface with business analysts, management, and other technology groups across WFS Responsibilities include development and maintenance of complex Application and supporting the application as an escalation point

Role and Responsibilities

Responsible for providing business analysis expertise for technology initiatives in support of a business desk and/or systems application(s) within the Securities businesses. Act as a liaison between the business and technology teams to interpret and develop requirements for business problems and recommend applications development / enhancement solutions.Use capital markets and technology application(s) knowledge to conduct detailed analysis in response to business &technology requests to develop efficient and accurate functional requirements. Coordinate communication and information flow between business and technology teams.
Create and maintain project / systems documentation and proactively monitor & manage project schedules and deliverables.
Work with business users to develop and provide training, resolve questions, assess user needs and recommend changes.
Participate in system testing and project development lifecycle management.
Manage team members
Knowledge of SEC, FINRA, Dodd Frank, Basel, and/or other industry regulations in developing technological solutions
