Who you are
As Change Manager, you are responsible to ensure that the Change Management process is executed and accountable for day-to-day management while upholding the integrity of the process. You are the main coordinator within this process and also the focal point regarding changes for the IT organization.
If you thrive in a dynamic, reciprocal workplace, IBM provides an environment where you will be challenged and inspired every single day. And if you relish the freedom to bring creative, thoughtful solutions to the table, there's no limit to what you can accomplish here.

Required Technical and Professional Expertise

5+ years of shown experience in Change Management domain
Experience in taking corrective action or bringing up in case of issues with the interfaced processes, Release Management, and Deployment Management
Validated expertise in chairing the Change Advisory Board (CAB) and Emergency meetings (ECAB)
Ability to prepare list of successful and failed changes closed for review at CAB meeting
Proficient in preparing the CAB meeting, including inviting of participants, creating of agenda, creation and circulation of Forward Schedule of Change and Projected Service Outage (PSO) - as applicable
Demonstrated ability in reviewing all applicable changes along with authorized changes are effectively communicated in a timely manner
Willingness to be ready and present Change Authorization Package to client partners
