Job Description
Job brief
We are looking for a Customer Relationship Manager to acquire, build and maintain long-term relationships with customers, to ensure their continued allegiance to the company and its products or services. Deal with people of diverse backgrounds and temperaments, think on their feet, and maintain full knowledge of the products or services the company offers. Should be face of the company to clients and customers.
Responsibilities
Send emails, letters, and other correspondence regarding problem resolutions, product and service features and upgrades, company changes, and other materials for new and existing customers.
Address all the enquiries on admin dashboard and take necessary action on the same
Manage inbound and outbound calls in a timely manner
Follow communication “scripts” when handling different topics
Identify customers’ needs, clarify information, research every issue and provide solutions and/or alternatives
Prompt and successful responses to customer and client issues
Share input on product development, sale and based on conversations with existing customers
Seize opportunities to upsell products when they arise
Build sustainable relationships and engage customers by taking the extra mile
Keep records of all conversations in our call centre database in a comprehensible way
Meet personal/team qualitative and quantitative targets
Follow the given KRA and ensure to achieve the same
Requirements
Previous experience in a sales/customer relationship role
Excellent communication skills along with active listening
Graduate (BBA,B.Com,BA )
Customer focus and adaptability to different personality types
Ability to multi-task, set priorities and manage time effectively
2-5 years of experience
