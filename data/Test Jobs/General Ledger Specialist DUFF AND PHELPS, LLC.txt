Duff and Phelps is hiring an Associate, as an Associate, you will be reporting to India controller and providing support to the GL reconciliation team for all accounting and reconciliation work to be performed supporting D&P global entities. The role will be responsible for the timely and accurate completion of all reconciliations assigned within the accounting function. You will be motivated to achieve individual, group and company goals.

At Duff & Phelps, your work will help protect, restore and maximize value for our clients. Join us and together we’ll maximize the value of your career.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
Execution of Reconciliations Assigned (Monthly/Weekly) for various entities across global
Following up & working on resolving outstanding items from Reconciliations
Research and reconcile differences for all accounts; gain an accurate understanding of & perform accounting research for complex transactions
Prepare Detailed Analytics for various Reconciliations items and getting them resolved
Actively participate in the overall close of the reconciliation (weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual) process.
Area of initial focus: Bank reconciliations and other GL reconciliation as necessary
Work independently to accomplish tasks; interact & work extensively with global finance teams to resolve issues
Assist with the external audit
Monthly variance analysis and follow-up
Perform other related duties as directed by the Head of Corporate Accounting
Identify potential process improvements and efficiencies
Assisting the team to ensure that accounting processes and procedures are followed and completed to support and reinforce key business objectives
Prepare Weekly Reconciliations for Key Accounts
REQUIREMENTS:
Minimum of 3-4 years of accounting experience, preferably in a services company environment
Bachelor’s degree in finance or accounting (B.Com/M.Com)
In-depth knowledge & understanding of Account Reconciliations & General Ledger Accounting
Proficient in Microsoft Excel
Ability to multi-task & support the team
Understanding of Accounts Payable & Receivables, Financial Systems and the ability to extract data from those systems
Experience working with project deadlines
High level of attention to detail & ability to research accounting for transactions
Excellent written, verbal and interpersonal skills
ERP experience, Lawson preferred
