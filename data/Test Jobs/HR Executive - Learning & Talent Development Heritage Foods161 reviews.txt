Job Summary
Learning and Talent Development has been one of the key HR functions to drive business and talent strategy in Heritage Foods Limited. Talent Management Lead to plan, design, develop and drive execution of L&D programs across all the HFL locations and to build healthy learning culture for Heritage Foods Limited organization.

Roles & Responsibilities:
Prioritize, manage, and tracking all the L&TD initiatives and programs.
Manage strong relationship with business sponsors and subject matter experts.
Implement communication plans (including both push and pull communication channels) with business leaders to build awareness of various avenues to build capability, such as training, assets and collaboration (leverage Heritage Learning).
Develop and implement learning assets and leadership programs designed to build the necessary skills (leverage Heritage learning where appropriate)
Define curriculum that meets business requirements and is aligned with training financial guidelines
Determine whether new assets should be built in-house or procured from external vendors.
Track and analyze training expenses against pre-defined budget.
Responsible and accountable for service delivery of capability/leadership development programs being delivered across entities and support the actions taken to improve overall quality and effectiveness.
Lead the performance management cycle (PMS) right from KRA-setting, performance and leadership competency review, normalization and increment and promotion cycles
