Basic Function
This position supports and assists the implementation of HCM (Human Capital Management) and Payroll System for Emerson Business Units in India, multi-Business Group environment. This position provides assistance to the Project Managers in HRIS for implementations and other enhancements. In addition, this position will help the Operations team in resolving production issues, as needed.
Principal Functional Responsibilities
Manage the configuration of the application based on Business Unit requirements and Emerson Global HRIS standards for Human Resources, Payroll, Benefits, Talent Acquisition, L&D and other associated modules
Coordinate with cross-functional teams in different lines of business to collect requirements for Oracle HCM and payroll vendor
Assist in Core HR and Payroll implementations in the following areas:
Participate in requirements gathering sessions and maintain requirements documents
Help in documenting the requirements, doing setups and testing the system
Work with the payroll vendor on interface.
Maintain other project management documentation such as fit/gap analysis
Participate in data validation
Assist with payroll parallel testing
Maintain reporting standards and coordinate reporting requirements across multiple Business Units
Provide support to the Business Units for HR, Payroll, Benefits, Talent Acquisition, L&D and other associated modules
Research new Oracle functionality to enhance customer usage of Oracle and assist in testing the new configuration before it is migrated in production
Assist with the maintenance of end-user training documentation
Provide support in resolving production issues and running India payroll operations.
Other duties as assigned

Education
Requires completion of a four-year bachelor’s degree in a Human Resources, Information Technology Business, or related field or equivalent experience.

Experience/Skills

Minimum of three years of related work experience configuration, implementation or business support experience with an Oracle EBS or Fusion HCM Cloud applications. Oracle HR, Payroll and/or Advanced Benefits experience preferred, however PeopleSoft or similar system experience will be considered
Good business knowledge in HR, Payroll, Benefits and related processes
Knowledge of PL/SQL, HCM Extracts, HDL and BI Reports is a plus.
Deadline oriented with a sense of urgency in meeting project goals
Ready and willing to share information, as necessary
Able to communicate with personnel in multi-disciplines from the clerical level to senior management
Able to interact professionally with clients, contractors and vendors both internal and external
Experience with large change initiatives is a plus
Interacts with other team members to exchange information and creates a positive and productive team dynamic
Ability to effectively work in a team environment
Excellent organizational and interpersonal and written communication skills
