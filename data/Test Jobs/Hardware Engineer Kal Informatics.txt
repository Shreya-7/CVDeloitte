Job Description for Hardware Engineer
We are looking for Hardware Engineer who is ready to meet daily challenges with
innovative problem-solving and forward-thinking solutions and participate in all phases of
design process using your creativity to develop, test, modify and manufacture new
technologies.
Responsibilities:
➢ Design and oversee the production of computer hardware equipment

➢ Test existing hardware components, including processors, circuit boards, network
equipment, computer chips, keyboards, routers, and printers, to define operational
parameters and recommend necessary upgrades and replacements to meet current
and future performance standards
➢ Perform routine maintenance on equipment and determine the type of tools needed
for repair
➢ Design support peripherals, including central processing units (CPUs), support logic,
microprocessors, custom integrated circuits, and printers and disk drives
➢ Analyse information and recommend appropriate hardware to users
➢ Update systems by improving the components, layout and design of a computer for
increased efficiency
➢ Supervises the manufacturing, production, and installation of the parts
➢ Dissembling the hardware components of system
➢ Laptop troubleshooting.

Skills and Competency Requirements:

➢ Bachelor’s degree in computer engineering or related field with 1 to 5 years of

experience
➢ Self-motivated with exceptional critical thinking and analytical abilities

➢ Innovative, results-oriented problem solver

➢ Knowledge of circuit boards, processors, chips and other electronic equipment

➢ Strong work ethic
Job Location: Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh
