Expectations
Operations bookkeeping and accounting systems to record accounting transactions on Tally (or any other tool) and preparing bank, foreign currency, and petty cash reconciliations.

Knowledge of accounting principles and practices.
Proficiency in Tally (latest version).
Proficiency with Email and Microsoft Office applications (Excel, Word, and PowerPoint).
Ability to meet assigned deadlines.
Good communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal skills.
