What is the Manager-Accounts Receivable in Global Accounts Receivable group responsible for?
The Manager, Global Accounts Receivable (AR) serves as the primary contact with senior management and functional owners within the revenue process, including departments such as Client Service Operations, Business Administration Operations, and Corporate Accounting. The primary responsibility is to lead and manage all aspects of the accounts receivable and revenue recognition process globally. The position is also responsible for ensuring that client needs for services are met or exceeded and that the department continues to evolve in efficiencies and effectiveness to meet client’s evolving needs and overall Company objectives.
