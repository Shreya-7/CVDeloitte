JD: - Packet Core Design Engineer

Responsibilities: -

The vEPC Engineer is mainly accountable for the timely development and delivery of complex engineering design and support deliveries, focusing mainly on the delivery of the customer on design, integration and testing activities.

Technical Skills Required:
Strong telecom fundamentals

Solid understanding of Mobile Packet Core, IP and VAS networks

Highly skilled in Network planning, Design and integration of Packet Core nodes.

Good understanding of technical specifications (like 3GPP, LTE) relevant to CS/PS Domain.

Good Knowledge of protocols like TCP/IP, PPP, AAA, OSPF, GTP & Diameter.

Good knowledge in capacity dimensioning and optimization.
