PatientMD is an Integrated Mobile Platform for information sharing and healthcare services. Our focus is to move the healthcare to a Patient-Centered Value-Based industry providing Precision Medicine and Personalized Healthcare. We have built 3 commercial apps on our platform for: Patients, Doctors and healthcare businesses. Our fourth app is used internally for our sales and customer support team.The foundation of our platform is our Patient Personal Health Record (PHR) and our interoperable consolidated Electronic Health Record (EHR) built on the FHIR protocol that can also interoperate with existing EHRs. We have built a number of services and technologies that utilize the data in our PHR, such as: Genomics (sequencing and biomarker identification), Artificial Intelligence, IoT, Telemedicine, Healthcare Services Marketplace, Healthcare Tourism and Appointment Scheduling. Our objective is to merge clinical, genomic and IoT data and use predictive analytics and AI to educate the patient and their doctor on Precision Medicine and Personalized Healthcare. As patients become engaged and empowered, they can then utilize our Marketplace for purchasing the desired services where price and quality transparency exists, creating an Amazon like experience. Patients can also use our Healthcare Tourism section to search domestically or internationally for the services.
