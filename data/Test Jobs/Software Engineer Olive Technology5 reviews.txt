On the most basic level, software engineers write, debug, maintain, and test software that instructs a computer to accomplish certain tasks, such as saving information, performing calculations, etc. A software engineer will convert what needs to happen into one of many programming languages, the most common of which being Java, C++, and Python.While many are self-taught, most software engineers have obtained an undergraduate or graduate degree in computer science or computer engineering.
