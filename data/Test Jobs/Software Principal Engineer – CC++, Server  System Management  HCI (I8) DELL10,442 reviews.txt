Key Responsibilities:
Interlocking with marketing, and other dependent teams to understand the scope of releases, change requests and customer issues to address in the server management product.
Own and drive design for complex features.
Offering impact analysis on our products from new technologies, competition / partner changes
Participate in architectural and design reviews during concept and development phases. Participate in engineering reviews to ensure engineering process rigor during development
Working with Advanced Engineering and Solution Product Marketing to define Engineering Requirements for PowerTools team portfolio.
Scoping for any new release: Risk assessment, resource/size estimation, identifying all dependencies, etc.
Technical guidance to development team on any evaluation, feasibility study or prototyping activity
Assisting development manager in technical mentorship of the team members
Providing technical guidance to junior developers in the team and mentoring them
Actively participating in all design and code review sessions within the team
Conducting deep-dive and training sessions to both internal and external teams
Keep abreast of and contribute towards emerging system management and technology trends in the industry
Essential Requirements:
Extensive working experience of programming in C/C++ on Linux flavors and Windows environments.
Strong knowledge of oops concept and usage, design patterns and experience in system level programming
In-depth experience in debugging complex code and use case scenarios
Experience working on Systems Management Software, Layered application development, third party libraries, and development & debug tools
Should have thorough experience on crash and core dump analysis
Knowledge of one or more of Systems Management standards like WSMAN, IPMI, SMASH, SMIS, and REDFISH is an added advantage
Knowledge of Servers and/or PC architecture required
Should be able to work in Agile methodologies
Knowledge of Virtualization in general and VMware suite of products around virtualization is desirable
Practicing DevOps model and have good understanding of build process and scripts

Excellent written and oral communication skills.
