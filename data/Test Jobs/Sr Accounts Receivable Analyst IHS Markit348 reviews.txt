Position summary
The high level purpose of the role is to ensure the timely and appropriate allocation of customer payments and to resolve payment discrepancies.
Candidates will need to have proven experience of previous cash allocation activity including account reconciliations, dealing with payment enquiries and resolving miss-allocations in a complex and multi-currency environment
What You'll Do
Allocating client payments in SAP in line with business process and client instructions
Escalating miss-allocations and completing account reconciliations to resolve
Escalating under payments/partial payments for corrective collections activity
Investigating and resolving 'un-known' payments received across all IHS Markit accounts and working with bankers to identify originator
Managing and coding FX losses and bank fees to the correct GL accounts in line with business policy and process
Liaising with collections teams to resolve unidentified payments and further allocating correctly.
Completing inter-company transfers as appropriate
Processing customer refunds and obtaining appropriate approvals in line with policy and process
Reporting and escalating payment exceptions
Reducing unallocated items in line with business objectives
Knowledge of Accounts Payable and T&E is added advantage
Supporting team members on a global basis in order to reduce debt and increase cash collection
Other ad hoc duties as required

What You Need to Get the Job Done
B.com/ MBA
Excellent systems knowledge including SAP Excel, Word and PowerPoint
Ability to operate at all levels of the organization
Proven experience of allocating customer payments in a collections environment is must.
Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal. Ability to communicate courteously and proactively within all levels of the organization
