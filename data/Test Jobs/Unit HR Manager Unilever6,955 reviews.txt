We are looking to hire a Unit HR Manager based in Pondicherry
Here is how your day at Unilever would look like:
Executing the Human Resource strategy for future business direction.
Executing strategies to engage union & union committee members to the optimum level.
Support in building a healthy working environment by creating sense of Belonging, Ownership & Care
Supporting in developing Negotiations strategy with team. Assisting in the Negotiation process.
Play vital role in all Conflict resolution pertaining to the workmen
Maintain cordial relations with all workmen by regular formal and informal interaction with workmen / workmen groups and opinion leaders on the shop floor.
Gauge and measure the IR climate of the unit with regular interactions with shop floor and line officers and managers.
Ensuring compliance and quality in performance & skill assessment of all workmen
Ensuring manpower availability of 100% at the shopfloor (permanent & contingent labour)
Tracking and controlling unauthorised absenteeism
Designing Annual Training Calendar, drafting monthly training plan & monitoring compliance
Mitigating Critical skill gaps and ensuring readiness on such identified critical skills.
Grievance Handling of workmen & Executives
Continuous tracking and elevating the employee participation and commitment in the engagement initiatives
Adhere to the unit IR strategy and help in avoiding any IR incident in the site
Key Skills & Requirement:
Must have the ability to go in details of every aspect, good at conceptual skills and thorough at people management techniques.
Should possess in-depth knowledge of labour laws and disciplinary proceedings along with its application and implementation in manufacturing facility.
Should be able to develop and execute various interventions required from time to time in context of changing organizational culture at unit level
Good in influencing skills and possess the maturity to handle a unionized workforce and establish a relationship of trust and transparency.
Ability to positively influence individual behaviours and positive discipline to establish a culture of mutual respect.
Experience in handling disciplinary proceedings, labour matters and unionized workforce is an added advantage
